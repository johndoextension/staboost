Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 27, 2025

StaBoost is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect your Instagram™ username, and only for the purpose of identifying and authenticating your session within the extension.

  • No passwords or private messages are collected.
  • No tracking or behavioral data is collected.
  • No data is shared with third parties.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

Your Instagram™ username is used solely to link your preferences (such as saved replies or tags) to your identity during use. This data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

3. Data Security

All data is handled securely and locally within your browser. StaBoost does not use remote databases or external servers.

4. Your Control

You can remove all locally stored data by uninstalling the extension or clearing your browser's local storage.

5. Compliance

This extension complies with Chrome Web Store policies and respects the privacy of Instagram™ users. It does not automate interactions or violate Instagram™’s terms of use.
